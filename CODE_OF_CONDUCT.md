# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and inclusive workplace across Higbee Associates repositories and related collaboration spaces.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the team and the product
- Showing empathy toward other community members and colleagues

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others’ private information without explicit permission
- Sharing credentials, client data, or other confidential information inappropriately
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered unprofessional in a workplace setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all Higbee Associates GitHub spaces and in other venues when an individual is representing Higbee Associates or its projects.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers or your manager / People Operations contact. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may take appropriate corrective action, including warning, temporary restriction, or permanent removal from project spaces.
